Kia Sportage is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and versatile driving experience. Since there are different model years and trim levels, the specifications can vary. The seats are designed for comfort, with options for power-adjustable and heated/ventilated seats in higher trims. - *Cargo space*: The cargo area provides ample space for luggage and other items. The rear seats can be folded down for additional cargo space. - *Technology*: Depending on the trim, the Kia Sportage comes equipped with an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system, Bluetooth connectivity, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto compatibility. Navigation, premium audio systems, and other tech features may be available on higher trims. - *Safety features*: The Kia Sportage is often equipped with a variety of safety features such as lane departure warning, blind-spot monitoring, forward collision warning, and automatic emergency braking. *Car Type:* - The Kia Sportage is classified as a compact SUV, which offers a balance of performance, versatility, and efficiency. - It is known for its smooth ride, comfortable cabin, and user-friendly technology.
